Usage Notes

Do use the primary (dark) version on Forest or white backgrounds. Use the reversed (light) version on Cream or very light backgrounds.

Don't recolour the icon, stretch the lockup, or place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid colour overlay.

Minimum size: 120px wide for the full lockup; 32px for the icon alone.

Clear space: Maintain clear space equal to the cap-height of the "N" on all sides of the lockup.
